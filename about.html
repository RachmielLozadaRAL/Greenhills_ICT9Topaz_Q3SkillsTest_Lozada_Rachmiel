About Employee Registration

This page provides information about the employee registration form. The form allows new employees to submit their details including name, age, contact number, email, username, and password. Upon submission, the user receives a confirmation alert indicating successful account creation.

The registration system is a simple client-side prototype meant for educational purposes and does not retain data. Additional validation or server-side processing can be implemented in a production environment.
